The Printing Press (1440)

Johannes Gutenberg invented the printing press, enabling the mass production of books.

Gutenberg printed the first book, The Latin Bible

First Newspaper (1690)

Benjamin Harris published Publick Occurrences Both Forreign and Domestick which is considered the the first newspaper

First Magazine (1731)

The Gentleman's Magazine became the first general magazine

First audio player (1877)

The Edison Phonograph was the first product designed to reproduce sound recordings.

Oldest Film (1888)

Roundhay Garden Scene became one of the first and the Oldest surviving motion films ever created

First Radio Station(1920)

Station KDKA became The First commercial broadcast. Which was used to get the election results to the population

First Televison(1927)

The world's first electronic television was created by a 21 year old inventor named Philo Taylor Farnsworth

First Satellite (1957)

Soviet Russia launched Sputnik 1; the first artificial satellite to be put in orbit
